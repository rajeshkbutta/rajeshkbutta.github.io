Hello! My name is Rajesh Butta. I am an AI/ML Specialist, graduate student, and researcher dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technologies to create transformative solutions for healthcare and business. My journey began with a strong passion for healthcare, initially driving me toward medical school. Along the way, I discovered a profound appreciation for data-driven decision-making and realized that the fusion of technology and AI could create broader, systemic improvements in patient care and operational efficiencies. This revelation led me to transition into computer science, focusing on AI engineering to solve complex, real-world problems.

My work exemplifies this mission. I developed orgoVR, a mobile augmented reality tool designed to make organic chemistry education immersive and accessible. As a research assistant, I applied AI/ML models to supply chain logistics, bolstering the resilience of free medical clinics in the Greater Tampa Bay Area, particularly during the critical supply shortages of COVID-19. My ongoing research includes a publication under review for a high-performing model that emulates and understands the brain-body connection, pushing the boundaries of AI’s potential to deepen our understanding of human physiology.

I thrive on fostering growth, curiosity, and authenticity in all I do, with a commitment to responsibility, balance, and disciplined creativity. My belief is that society grows when individuals are empowered to lead richer, fuller lives, supported by technology that enhances, not distracts. By striving for technological innovations that reduce life's difficulties, I aim to create space for deeper human creativity and fulfillment.

In addition to my technical pursuits, I’ve led workshops, such as a talk on effective communication at the Florida International Leadership Conference, where I facilitated group activities to boost participants' confidence and presentation skills. My goal is to serve as a lead AI engineer, building domain-specific AI applications that bring meaningful improvements to business and healthcare.

In my free time, I prioritize personal growth through exercise and meditation, finding that balance and inner harmony are key to sustained creativity and resilience.
